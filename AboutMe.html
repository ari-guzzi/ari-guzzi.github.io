Hello! I'm Ari...

I'm pursuing a BS in Creative Technology and Design with a minor in Computer Science from the College of Engineering and Applied Science at the University of Colorado Boulder. My program combines creativity with engineering, focusing on critical thinking, problem-solving, design, and creative production.

During my time at CU Boulder, I've honed my ability to take constructive feedback and solve problems from diverse perspectives. Beyond coursework, I've delved deep into my passions; am a part of a university club, Blueprint Boulder, which builds apps and websites for nonprofits. I am the head of front-end design for a team developing a database for a nonprofit. This experience highlighted how technology fuels my creativity. I've also enjoyed blending skills from various classes to tackle multifaceted projects.

I'm passionate about mobile apps, websites, and UI/UX development. My Computer Science minor and extracurricular work have also made me confident in full-stack development.

I was born and raised in Boulder, Colorado. When not working, I enjoy gardening, building apothecaries, embroidering, volunteering, and playing with my dog!
