Hello! I'm Ari...

I'm pursuing a BS in Creative Technology and Design with a minor in Computer Science from the College of Engineering and Applied Science at the University of Colorado Boulder. This program blends creativity with an engineering curriculum, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, design, and creative production.

During my time at CU Boulder, I've not only sharpened my ability to receive and apply constructive criticism, but also honed my problem-solving skills, allowing me to approach challenges from diverse perspectives. Beyond coursework, I've delved deep into my passions, I am the head of front-end development for a team in Blueprint Boulder, a CU club that builds apps and websites for nonprofits. We developed a database for a nonprofit. This experience illuminated the pivotal role of technology in expressing my creativity. Additionally, I've learned to merge various technological skills from different classes to drive cohesive, multifaceted projects.

I'm particularly drawn to front-end development, finding joy in projects related to mobile app, website, and UI/UX development. Through my computer science minor and extracurriculars, I became comfortable with full-stack development.

I was born and raised in Boulder, Colorado. When I am not working, I thoroughly enjoy gardening, building apothecaries, embroidering, volunteering, and playing with my dog!
