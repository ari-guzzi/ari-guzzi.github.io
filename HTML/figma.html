App Design Projects

One of the projects I have been working on is a prototype app for the nonprofit, Natural Highs. I utilized Figma to make the prototype.
The app is a social media page for our nonprofit. You create an account where you input your information and demographics. You also signed the consent form for Acudetox. When you sign up, you automatically follow all the main pages (Boulder, Canada, Australia) and all the registered peer mentors from your community. After creating an account/ signing in, the app automatically loads to the feed page. From the feed page, you can navigate throughout the app. There is an events page you can scan a QR code and check in for the event. After checking in, there is a survey so we can collect the information needed for grants. The app will have a database holding the demographics of each user along with their answers to surveys.
