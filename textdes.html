Text Design Projects

I took a class that focused on the fundamentals of design, typography and layout within digital and analogue media.

Marketing for a Fictitious Product
For one project, I designed packaging for a health-focused vape aimed at assisting individuals in transitioning away from THC use. I curated a color palette, selected fonts, and developed a comprehensive marketing strategy for this fictitious product. I used Adobe Illustrator and Adobe InDesign for this project.

Mailer for the Colorado Symphony
For another project, I designed a mailer for the Colorado Symphony. This proved to be a challenging endeavor due to the dense amount of information that needed to be included. I strategically organized the content to ensure readability, using complementary colors to enhance the visual appeal. I used Adobe Illustrator for this project.

Creative Graphic Describing a Song
In addition, I designed a graphic to be printed on sturdy paper and folded into an accordion-style package. Using typography and layout techniques, I crafted visuals that encapsulated the essence of song lyrics. I selected 'Out of the Woods' by Taylor Swift as my inspiration.
