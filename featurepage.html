Natural Highs' App Figma

This prototype app is for Natural Highs, a nonprofit organization dedicated to teaching healthy alternatives to substances. This UI design was created in Figma to make the prototype.
The app is a social media page for the nonprofit. The user can create an account where they input their information and demographics. When they sign up, they automatically follow all the main Natural Highs' locations pages (Boulder, Canada, Australia) and all the registered peer mentors from their specific community. After signing in, the app automatically loads the feed page. From there, they can navigate throughout the app. There is an events page where you can scan a QR code to check in for each event. After checking in, users are asked to fill out a survey with the information needed for Natural Highs to apply for grants. The app has a database holding the demographics of each user along with their answers to surveys.
